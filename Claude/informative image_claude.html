Yosemite National Park

Yosemite National Park is one of America's most iconic natural treasures, located in California's Sierra Nevada mountains. Established in 1890, the park spans nearly 1,200 square miles of breathtaking landscapes.

Yosemite is renowned for its waterfalls, giant sequoias, vast wilderness, and diverse ecosystems. The park attracts millions of visitors each year who come to experience its natural beauty and recreational opportunities.
