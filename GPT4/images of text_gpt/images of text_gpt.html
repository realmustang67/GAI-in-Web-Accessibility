What is the Web Accessibility Initiative?

The Web Accessibility Initiative (WAI), developed by the W3C, provides strategies, guidelines, and resources to make the web accessible to people with disabilities. WAI is an international effort with support from industry, disability organizations, governments, and research labs.

Learn more at w3.org/WAI .
